Serving attorneys across Nebraska

Purinton Analytics provides Nebraska trial attorneys and insurers with vocational expert evaluations and life care plans that translate injury, disability, and loss into clear, defensible opinions. We are retained by both plaintiff and defense counsel and provide testimony in Nebraska district courts and before the Nebraska Workers' Compensation Court.

Every earning capacity and employability opinion is grounded in Nebraska's labor market — local wages, job availability, and occupational requirements — so conclusions reflect the economy your case will be tried in, not national averages.

Vocational expert & life care planning FAQs — Nebraska

Do you provide vocational expert services throughout Nebraska?

Yes. Purinton Analytics provides vocational expert evaluations, earning capacity and wage loss analysis, and life care planning for cases throughout Nebraska — from Omaha and Lincoln to smaller communities across the state — for both plaintiff and defense counsel.

Are your opinions admissible in Nebraska district courts?

Our opinions are built on accepted vocational and life care planning methodology and documented data so they withstand scrutiny in Nebraska district courts and before the Nebraska Workers' Compensation Court, including Daubert and Frye challenges.

Do you handle Nebraska workers' compensation matters?

Yes. We provide employability evaluations, transferable skills analysis, and labor market research for matters before the Nebraska Workers' Compensation Court, in addition to personal injury, employment, and family law cases.

Tell us about your Nebraska matter and we'll outline how an objective vocational or life care planning opinion can support it.
