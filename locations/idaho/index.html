Serving attorneys across Idaho

Purinton Analytics provides Idaho trial attorneys and insurers with vocational expert evaluations and life care plans that translate injury, disability, and loss into clear, defensible opinions. We are retained by both plaintiff and defense counsel and provide testimony in Idaho district courts and before the Idaho Industrial Commission.

Every earning capacity and employability opinion is grounded in Idaho's labor market — local wages, job availability, and occupational requirements — so conclusions reflect the economy your case will be tried in, not national averages.

Vocational expert & life care planning FAQs — Idaho

Do you provide vocational expert services throughout Idaho?

Yes. Purinton Analytics provides vocational expert evaluations, earning capacity and wage loss analysis, and life care planning for cases throughout Idaho — from Boise and Meridian to smaller communities across the state — for both plaintiff and defense counsel.

Are your opinions admissible in Idaho district courts?

Our opinions are built on accepted vocational and life care planning methodology and documented data so they withstand scrutiny in Idaho district courts and before the Idaho Industrial Commission, including Daubert and Frye challenges.

Do you handle Idaho workers' compensation matters?

Yes. We provide employability evaluations, transferable skills analysis, and labor market research for matters before the Idaho Industrial Commission, in addition to personal injury, employment, and family law cases.
