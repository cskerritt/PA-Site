Serving attorneys across Missouri

Purinton Analytics provides Missouri trial attorneys and insurers with vocational expert evaluations and life care plans that translate injury, disability, and loss into clear, defensible opinions. The firm is based in Kansas City, Missouri, and works with counsel throughout the state. We are retained by both plaintiff and defense counsel and provide testimony in Missouri circuit courts and before the Missouri Division of Workers' Compensation.

Every earning capacity and employability opinion is grounded in Missouri's labor market — local wages, job availability, and occupational requirements — so conclusions reflect the economy your case will be tried in, not national averages.

Vocational expert & life care planning FAQs — Missouri

Do you provide vocational expert services throughout Missouri?

Yes. Purinton Analytics provides vocational expert evaluations, earning capacity and wage loss analysis, and life care planning for cases throughout Missouri — from Kansas City and St. Louis to smaller communities across the state — for both plaintiff and defense counsel.

Are your opinions admissible in Missouri circuit courts?

Our opinions are built on accepted vocational and life care planning methodology and documented data so they withstand scrutiny in Missouri circuit courts and before the Missouri Division of Workers' Compensation, including Daubert and Frye challenges.

Do you handle Missouri workers' compensation matters?

Yes. We provide employability evaluations, transferable skills analysis, and labor market research for matters before the Missouri Division of Workers' Compensation, in addition to personal injury, employment, and family law cases.

Tell us about your Missouri matter and we'll outline how an objective vocational or life care planning opinion can support it.
