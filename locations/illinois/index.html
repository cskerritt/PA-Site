Serving attorneys across Illinois

Purinton Analytics provides Illinois trial attorneys and insurers with vocational expert evaluations and life care plans that translate injury, disability, and loss into clear, defensible opinions. We are retained by both plaintiff and defense counsel and provide testimony in Illinois circuit courts and before the Illinois Workers' Compensation Commission.

Every earning capacity and employability opinion is grounded in Illinois's labor market — local wages, job availability, and occupational requirements — so conclusions reflect the economy your case will be tried in, not national averages.

Vocational expert & life care planning FAQs — Illinois

Do you provide vocational expert services throughout Illinois?

Yes. Purinton Analytics provides vocational expert evaluations, earning capacity and wage loss analysis, and life care planning for cases throughout Illinois — from Chicago and Aurora to smaller communities across the state — for both plaintiff and defense counsel.

Are your opinions admissible in Illinois circuit courts?

Our opinions are built on accepted vocational and life care planning methodology and documented data so they withstand scrutiny in Illinois circuit courts and before the Illinois Workers' Compensation Commission, including Daubert and Frye challenges.

Do you handle Illinois workers' compensation matters?

Yes. We provide employability evaluations, transferable skills analysis, and labor market research for matters before the Illinois Workers' Compensation Commission, in addition to personal injury, employment, and family law cases.

Tell us about your Illinois matter and we'll outline how an objective vocational or life care planning opinion can support it.
