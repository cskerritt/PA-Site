Serving attorneys across Kansas

Purinton Analytics provides Kansas trial attorneys and insurers with vocational expert evaluations and life care plans that translate injury, disability, and loss into clear, defensible opinions. We are retained by both plaintiff and defense counsel and provide testimony in Kansas district courts and before the Kansas Division of Workers Compensation.

Every earning capacity and employability opinion is grounded in Kansas's labor market - local wages, job availability, and occupational requirements - so conclusions reflect the economy your case will be tried in, not national averages.

Vocational expert & life care planning FAQs - Kansas

Do you provide vocational expert services throughout Kansas?

Yes. Purinton Analytics provides vocational expert evaluations, earning capacity and wage loss analysis, and life care planning for cases throughout Kansas - from Wichita and Overland Park to smaller communities across the state - for both plaintiff and defense counsel.

Are your opinions admissible in Kansas district courts?

Our opinions are built on accepted vocational and life care planning methodology and documented data so they withstand scrutiny in Kansas district courts and before the Kansas Division of Workers Compensation, including Daubert and Frye challenges.

Do you handle Kansas workers' compensation matters?

Yes. We provide employability evaluations, transferable skills analysis, and labor market research for matters before the Kansas Division of Workers Compensation, in addition to personal injury, employment, and family law cases.

Need a vocational or life care expert in Kansas?

Tell us about your Kansas matter and we'll outline how an objective vocational or life care planning opinion can support it.
